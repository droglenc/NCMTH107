Drinking Water Source

The approximate frequency table is shown below.

      Groundwater    Lake/Reservoir             River           Muliple 
              119               219                72               118 
       Don't Know Refused to Answer 
              221                 2

A very large number of respondents did not know where their water supply was derived. Of those that did, most were derived from a lake or reservoir, with about the same number evenly split between groundwater and multiple sources.

Curbside Recycling

More than half of the respondents felt that the curbside recycling program has reduced their waste by approximately 25%, with approximately another third saying approximately 50% (Table 1). The only other response offered was “too early to tell.”

Table 1: Percentage table for how much curbside recycling has reduced overall refuse. Note that B=25%, C=50%, and E=‘too early to tell’.

recycle
   B    C    E 
55.6 36.5  7.9

R Code Appendix

library(NCStats)
df <- read.csv("data/Recycling.csv")
t.hi <- xtabs(~recycle,data=df)
percTable(t.hi,digits=1)
