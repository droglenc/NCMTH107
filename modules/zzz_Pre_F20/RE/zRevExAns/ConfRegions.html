BOD in Effluent

  1. 90% (=100(1-0.10))
  2. Lower confidence bound (because HA is “greater than”).
  3. Z*=-1.282
  4. 2504-1.282\(\frac{1200}{\sqrt(118)}\) = 2362.
  5. I am 90% confident that the population mean BOD is greater than 2362. By confident I mean that 90% of all 90% confidence regions will contain the true population mean.
