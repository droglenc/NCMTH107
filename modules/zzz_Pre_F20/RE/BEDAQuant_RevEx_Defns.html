What would the form be called if the “cloud” of data looks like an exponential function?

Nonlinear

What would the form be called if the “cloud” of data is not obviously curves?

Linear

What type of association shows no tendency for above average values of one variable to be with above or below average values of the other variable?

No association

What type of association has a scatterplot with points that generally increase from the lower-left to the upper-right?

Positive association

What type of association has a scatterplot with points that look completely randomly scattered?

No association

What type of association shows a general tendency for above average values of one variable to be with above average values of the other variable (and, thus, below average for one also tends to be below average for the other)?

Positive association

What type of association shows a general tendency for above average values of one variable to be with below average values of the other variable?

Negative association

What type of association has a scatterplot with points that generally decrease from the upper-left to the lower-right?

Positive association

What does the sign of the correlation coefficient tell us?

Tells about association – positive r is positive association, negative r is negative association, and an r of zero is no association.

When should the correlation coefficient NOT be used to assess strength?

When the form is nonlinear or outliers are present.

What word would be used to describe the strength of the linear relationship in an uncontrolled study if r=0.70?

Moderate strength

What word would be used to describe the strength of the linear relationship in a controlled study if r=0.94?

Moderate strength
