Background

Erickson et al. (2004) compared the bite-force performance between American Alligators (Alligator mississippiensis) from the wild and those that had been in long-term captivity. In one aspect of their research they examined the relationship between the mass (kg) and snout-vent length (svl; cm) of the alligators. Their primary interest here was in determining if variability in the mass could be explained by knowing the snout-vent length of the individual. The data are recorded in Alligators1.csv. Use these data to describe the relationship between the mass and snout-vent length of the alligators.
