68-95-99.7 (Empirical) Rule

You can click on each question to see an answer. Click on it again to close the answer.

On any normal distribution, what percentage of individuals is within ±1σ of μ?

68%

On any normal distribution, what percentage of individuals is within ±3σ of μ?

99.7%

On any normal distribution, what percentage of individuals is greater than μ+σ?

16%

On any normal distribution, what percentage of individuals is less than μ-2σ?

2.5%

On any normal distribution, what percentage of individuals is between μ-2σ and μ+σ?

81.5% (using the results from the previous two questions … 100-16-2.5).

On a N(1,1), what percentage of individuals are negative?

16% (Note that negative means less than 0, 0 is one σ below μ on this distribution, and 16% of individuals are less than one σ below μ.

On a N(100,20), what percentage of individuals are less than 80?

16% (16% of individuals are less than one σ below μ.

On a N(-20,100), what percentage of individuals are greater than 80?

16% (16% of individuals are greater than one σ above μ.

On a N(50,20), what percentage of individuals are less than 60?

This question cannot be answered with the 68-95-99.7 Rule because 60 is not an integer number of σs away from μ.
