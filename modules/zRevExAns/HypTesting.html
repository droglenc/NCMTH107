BOD in Effluent

  1. \(H_{O}:\mu=2200\) vs. \(H_{O}:\mu>2200\).
  2. \(z=\frac{2504-2200}{\frac{1200}{\sqrt{118}}}=2.75\).
  3. The p-value is .
  4. The p-value is less than \(\alpha\), therefore \(H_{O}\) is rejected.
  5. There is evidence that the mean BOD level is greater than 2200 lbs/day. Thus, the plant managers will need to make corrective actions.
